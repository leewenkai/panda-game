Fascinating Panda Facts

Diet

Pandas eat 20-40 pounds of bamboo per day!

Habitat

Giant pandas live in temperate broadleaf and mixed forests in the mountains of southwest China.

Conservation Status

While still vulnerable, pandas have been upgraded from "endangered" thanks to conservation efforts.
